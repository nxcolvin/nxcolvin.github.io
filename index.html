Mindfulness For Stress Course

Juggling work and home life can be exhausting! Mindfulness has been proven scientifically to help with stress and anxiety, increasing well-being and improving heath. The Mindfulness for Stress course is designed to teach practical ways to deal with stress by allowing to respond rather than react to life's difficult situations. By using simple techniques, including breathing mediations, taught on this course you can train yourself to engage with everyday life in a Mindfull manner, becoming calmer and more grounded. 

This course is taught over eight weeks in weekly two hour group sessions, with a maximum of around eight participants.
